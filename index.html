About Me

I am a retired medical doctor with a PhD in visual physiology who initially trained as an optometrist.
I went on to begin training in ophthalmology before a brain tumour rudely interrupted me.
I changed specialties to histopathology/clinical pathology before having to retire on the grounds of ill-health.
Of late, I have been working towards a BSc(Hons) in Mathematics & Statistics through the Open University.
I am also studying the Full Stack Web Development Bootcamp, run by The App Brewery on Udemy.
